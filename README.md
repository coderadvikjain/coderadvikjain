### Glad to see you here!  
I'm Advik Jain — a full-stack developer & AI/ML enthusiast who has turned years of freelancing into a full-time career. I build both client-facing applications and the powerful backend + AI systems that support them.

I specialize in developing robust, scalable backends and intelligent AI-powered applications. I enjoy designing systems that are lightweight yet powerful, distributed yet synchronized, and visually appealing yet highly functional.

Currently, I'm diving deep into **Machine Learning & Deep Learning** — building models with PyTorch and scikit-learn — and exploring **Next.js** for modern frontend development. I'm also working on AI-driven applications that bring ML models to production.

I believe in building systems that scale seamlessly, integrating intelligence where it matters, and designing user experiences that leave a lasting impression.
